Croissant, the flaky and buttery delight that's named after its crescent-shaped pastry, resembling a crescent moon. This scrumptious treat originated in France, where it has become an iconic pastry loved by people all around the world. Indulge in its heavenly layers of dough and enjoy its rich and satisfying flavor that's perfect for any time of the day!

The origin of croissants is often debated, with both France and Austria claiming their invention. However, croissants are widely believed to have originated in France, where they have become an iconic pastry in French cuisine. The exact history and origin of croissants are complex, with various theories and stories, but they are commonly associated with France and are often considered a staple of French breakfasts and pastries.
